FAQ

How do I add a friend?

Share your PIN or QR code, or enter a friend's PIN to add them — no phone number needed.

I forgot my password

On the sign-in screen tap "Forgot password?", enter your email, and you'll get a reset link that opens the app directly.

Are my messages and calls really encrypted?

Yes — text and voice calls are end-to-end encrypted. Even we cannot read them. See the Privacy Policy.

I changed my device and want my chats back

Use the key backup phrase you saved during setup. Without it, older encrypted messages can't be recovered — that's part of the security model.

I want to delete my account

Email nahlah@nahlah.io and we'll delete your account and associated data.
